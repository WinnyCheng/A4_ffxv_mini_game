Choose Your Character

Choose a Final Fantasy Character of your choice: the muscular, all mighty shield Gladio Amicitia, the cool, intelligent adviser Ignis Scientia, the handsome, sleepy, young prince Noctis Lucis Caelum, and the cute, playfull photographer Prompto Argentum. Choose any character by clicking on his image. It will then bring you to a to another page where you can move the character around as you like using the w, a, s, d keys. Press j to attack and press k for a special attack. Press u and the character does a little action.
